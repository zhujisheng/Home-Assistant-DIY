## 连接蓝牙音箱

- 启动Add-on `Terminal & SSH`
- 运行`bluetoothctl`

    ```sh
    scan on
    pair <your mac address>
    trust <your mac address>
    connect <your mac address>
    discoverable on
    pairable on
    default-agent
    exit
    ```

- `pactl`命令

    `pactl list sinks short`
